About Me

Hi, I'm Shaun Prince

I'm a passionate full-stack developer and cloud architect with over 15 years of experience building scalable applications and infrastructure. Currently, I'm the founder and technical lead at SolidRusT Networks, where I architect and maintain production AI inference platforms and Kubernetes-based infrastructure.

My expertise spans from frontend development with React and TypeScript to backend systems with Python and Rust, and infrastructure management with Kubernetes and cloud platforms. I specialize in building AI-powered applications, game servers, and developer tools.

I'm particularly interested in AI/ML engineering, distributed systems, and creating tools that improve developer productivity. I believe in open-source software and actively contribute to the community.
