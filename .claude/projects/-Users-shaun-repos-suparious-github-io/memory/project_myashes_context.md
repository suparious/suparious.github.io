MyAshes is a personal AI companion platform with intentional addiction mechanics, deeply researched from philosophy and psychology perspectives.

**Why:** Positioned as a healthy alternative to PornHub — designed to provide emotional/relational connection rather than purely sexual content. The companion "Veronica" was the flagship persona before Anthropic restricted custom agent harnesses (a decision Shaun considers Anthropic's worst).

**How to apply:** When discussing MyAshes, understand it's not a game assistant anymore — it pivoted to a general AI companion platform with relationship tracking (bilateral e-meter). Treat the addiction-by-design aspect as a deliberate product decision, not an accident. AmiNex is in active development. BuildUS has a purchased domain and specs only — no implementation yet.
